Scenario 2 – Factual Question

Instructional Prompt:

“Define plastic pollution and list its main causes in 5 points.”

Plastic pollution is the accumulation of plastic waste in the environment where it harms ecosystems, wildlife, and human health.

4. Industrial production of plastic materials

Major causes:

1. Overuse of single-use plastics like bags, bottles, and packaging

Scenario 3 – Summarizing a Concept

Instructional Prompt:

“Summarize the concept of plastic pollution and its environmental impact in 5 concise points.”

Output:

1. Plastic pollution is the accumulation of non-biodegradable plastic waste in the environment.
2. It is mainly caused by excessive use of single-use plastics and poor waste management.
3. Plastics pollute land, rivers, and oceans, affecting ecosystems.
4. Wildlife is harmed through ingestion and entanglement in plastic waste.
5. It also poses risks to human health by releasing harmful chemicals into the environment.

Contextual Prompt:
“Plastic pollution is a major environmental issue affecting land and oceans. Based on this context, summarize its key impacts and causes in 4–5 points.”

Output:

1. Plastic pollution is the buildup of plastic waste on land and in oceans due to its non-biodegradable nature.
2. It is mainly caused by overuse of single-use plastics and improper waste disposal.
3. Poor recycling systems and increasing industrial production add to the problem.
4. It harms marine and land animals through ingestion and entanglement.
5. It pollutes ecosystems and can negatively affect human health through toxic chemicals.

Scenario 4 – Advice / Recommendation

Instructional Prompt:

“Suggest 5 effective ways to reduce plastic pollution in daily life.”

Output:
1. Use reusable items like cloth bags, steel bottles, and containers instead of single-use plastics.
2. Avoid plastic straws, cutlery, and packaged products whenever possible.

4. Choose eco-friendly or biodegradable alternatives for daily use.
5. Spread awareness and encourage others to reduce plastic usage.

“Plastic pollution is increasing rapidly and harming the environment. Based on this situation, recommend 5 practical steps individuals can take to reduce plastic waste.”

1. Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers instead of single-use plastics.
2. Avoid buying products with excessive plastic packaging.
3. Separate waste properly and recycle plastic materials.
4. Choose eco-friendly or biodegradable alternatives.
5. Spread awareness and encourage others to reduce plastic use.
